ABOUT THE MUSEUM

The Story of Civilization

Discover the vision behind the world’s largest archaeological museum, where history, culture, and innovation unite.

Who We Are

The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is a global cultural destination located near the Giza Pyramids. It showcases Egypt’s unparalleled heritage through an extraordinary collection of ancient artifacts, reflecting over 5,000 years of civilization.

GEM is designed as a bridge between the past and the future, combining cutting-edge museum technology with timeless history to offer visitors a unique and immersive experience.

Our Mission

Preserve, present, and promote Egypt’s cultural heritage for future generations.

Our Vision

To be a leading global museum that inspires curiosity, learning, and cultural dialogue.

Our Impact

Connecting people worldwide with the story of ancient Egypt through education and innovation.
