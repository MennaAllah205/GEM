Find the Grand Egyptian Museum

Located near the Giza Pyramids, the Grand Egyptian Museum is easily accessible from all major areas of Cairo.

How to Get There

The Grand Egyptian Museum is located on the Giza Plateau, just minutes away from the Pyramids of Giza. Visitors can reach the museum by car, taxi, or tour buses.

  • 30 minutes from Downtown Cairo
  • Public transport & tour buses available
  • 45 minutes from Cairo International Airport
